Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4
Parmesan cheese 50g
Garlic 2 cloves
Black pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.

Step 2: In a separate pan, fry pancetta with garlic until crispy.

Step 3: Beat the eggs and mix with parmesan and black pepper.

Step 4: Combine the pasta with the pancetta and egg mixture, toss to coat, and serve immediately.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 200g
Brown sugar 200g
Granulated sugar 100g
Eggs 2
Flour 300g
Chocolate chips 200g
Baking soda 1 tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.

Step 2: Cream the butter and sugars together in a bowl until smooth.

Step 3: Add eggs one at a time, then stir in the flour and baking soda.

Step 4: Fold in chocolate chips and scoop dough onto the tray.

Step 5: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.

Source: AllRecipes
